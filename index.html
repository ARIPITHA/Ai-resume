Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the OneStep Resume AI platform, templates, and billing.

How does the ATS compatibility score work?

Our algorithm scans your resume template structure and matches your keywords against the job description using a TF-IDF text density calculator. It also checks formatting elements like font sizes, paragraph lengths, and table usage to score compatibility between 0 and 100.

Will my data be used to train AI models?

Absolutely not. We have strict privacy policies in place. Your resumes, project descriptions, cover letters, and chat records are stored locally or in encrypted databases, and are never used to train Anthropic, OpenAI, or other foundational language models.

Can I export my resume to Microsoft Word (DOCX)?

Yes. The platform supports exporting your resume files as pixel-perfect PDFs, standard JSON schema data exports, and fully editable Word files (DOCX) matching the selected layout structure.

How accurate is the AI Career Coach?

The AI Career Coach uses deep contextual history to evaluate your work experience and target companies. It is highly accurate for formatting tips, mock behavioral preparation, and identifying key technical tools missing from your profile.

Help Center

Getting Started

How to create your first resume

To create your first resume, navigate to the Resume Builder section in the sidebar. Fill in your personal information, work experience, education, and skills. Use the AI Enhance button to improve your bullet points, then export to Word or PDF.

Understanding ATS scoring

ATS scoring analyzes how well your resume matches job descriptions. Use the ATS Analyzer to paste job descriptions and get a compatibility score with keyword matching and improvement suggestions.

Account & Billing

Upgrade to Premium

Premium members get unlimited AI enhancements, cover letter generator, custom roadmaps, and all designer templates. Click the Upgrade to Pro button in the header to upgrade your account.

Manage subscription

Manage your subscription from your profile settings. You can view billing history, change plans, or cancel your subscription at any time.

Technical Support

Export issues

If you're having trouble exporting, try using a different browser or clearing your cache. For persistent issues, contact support at support@onestep.ai

Template customization
